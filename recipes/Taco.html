Ground Beef Taco Recipe

Description

These crispy ground beef tacos are filled with seasoned meat, lettuce, cheese and tomatoes. An easy dinner recipe that the whole family will enjoy, even your pickiest eaters! Includes a recipe for ground beef taco seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Lean ground bee
  • Onion finely chopped
  • Chili powder
  • Onion powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground cumin
  • Taco shells
  • Sour cream
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh toppings include diced tomatoes, avocado or Guacamole, sliced jalapeños, red onions or scallions, chopped cilantro, and shredded iceberg lettuce

Step by step

  1. Warm up the tortillas, and prep all your ingredients and toppings.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up as you go along.
  3. Add in the seasoning mix, and cook until the meat is cooked through. If there’s any excess fat, drain it. Taste the beef, and add salt & pepper if desired.
  4. Assemble the tacos right away, and enjoy!
