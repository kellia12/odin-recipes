Tiramisu Cake Recipe

Description

This simple tiramisu slab cake not only feeds a crowd, but itâ€™s much easier to prepare than a regular tiramisu.

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp instant espresso powder
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 3 x 230g unfilled sponge cakes
  • 600ml cream
  • 250g mascarpone
  • 1/2 tin condensed milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  • Chocolate, for grating

Step by step

  1. Combine espresso powder with boiling water and set aside to cool a little.
  2. In an approx. 32cm x 22cm baking dish, place a single layer of sponge cake, cutting it to fit. Drizzle over cooled espresso liquid.
  3. In a stand mixer or using handheld beaters, whip cream, mascarpone, condensed milk and vanilla until thick. Spread in a single layer over sponge cake. Refrigerate for one hour to set and chill. Grate over dark chocolate. Slice into squares and serve.
