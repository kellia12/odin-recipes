Lasagna Recipe

Description

Homemade lasagna may have a few steps, but each step is easy and I assure you it is worth the time; the perfect Italian meal! The best lasagna recipe is made with ingredients that you know, and it is not difficult at all! All you will need for this easy lasagna recipe is one pan, one bowl, and a 9×13 baking dish!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • onions
  • Red wine or beef broth
  • Garlic cloves
  • Marinara sauce
  • Dried thyme
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg

Step by step

  1. Brown the beef: Add oil to a deep pan and sautee onion and beef until browned, 5 minutes then add garlic and stir another minute.
  2. Finish the sauce: Pour in 1/4 cup of wine and stir until almost evaporated. Add marinara, salt, pepper, thyme, sugar, and parsley; bring to a simmer, then cover and cook 5 minute
  3. Make the cheese fillingMake the cheese filling: Combine all of the cheese sauce ingredients and 1 cup of mozzarella in a mixing bowl (reserve the rest for later).
  4. Prep: Preheat your oven to 375ºF and cook the lasagna noodles in a pot of well-salted water until al dente.
  5. Assemble: Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9×13-inch casserole dish. Add 3 noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1 cup of mozzarella, and half of the ricotta mixture. Repeat, then for the top layer, add 3 noodles, 1/3 of the meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375˚F for 45 minutes, then remove the foil and broil for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the cheese is lightly browned.
  7. Rest: Let the lasagna rest for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.
